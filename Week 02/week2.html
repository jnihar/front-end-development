Description

Mango

A mango is a sweet, tropical fruit, often called the "king of fruits." It's the national fruit of India, known for its juicy, pulpy flesh, and is enjoyed fresh or in various dishes.

Apple

An apple is a round, edible fruit produced by apple trees. It comes in various colors, such as red and green, with a sweet or tart taste. Apples are widely cultivated and enjoyed fresh or in various dishes, known for their health benefits.

Banana

A banana is a long, edible fruit produced by herbaceous plants in the genus Musa. It's a berry and a significant crop worldwide, known for its sweet taste, yellow color, and versatility in culinary uses.

Strawberry

A strawberry is a fruit from the Fragaria genus. It's not a true berry but an "accessory fruit" with a fleshy receptacle. Strawberries are widely cultivated, known for their juicy, succulent, and fragrant qualities

Peach

A peach is a round fruit with sweet, juicy, yellow flesh surrounded by slightly furry red and yellow skin. It belongs to the Prunus persica species and is related to plums, cherries, and almonds, known for its delicious taste and large seed in the center

Lychee

A lychee is a subtropical fruit known for its sweet, juicy aril. It grows on a tall evergreen tree with pink-red, rough-textured shells. Native to Southeast Asia, lychee is prized for its delicious flavor and is commonly eaten fresh
